Engineering Purpose

J.P. Samuel & Associates Inc. brings together an experienced team of professionals who design and deliver innovative and practical solutions to meet any vision. We understand that our customer’s needs are diverse and challenging, hence we leverage efficiency through a network of strategic alliances with experts in complimentary fields. We work with an extremely dedicated team of architects, engineers, consultants and contractors to ensure the most adaptive and comprehensive solutions are delivered to you within your time and cost constraints. 


At J.P. Samuel, we have adopted a team approach on all of our projects. We take an active leadership role in design development and work closely with the project team to ensure that our clients receive full value from our services.

Strong Foundations

J.P. Samuel and Associates Inc. was founded in 2007 by James Samuel P.Eng. with the objective of providing the structural engineering services to exceed our client's vision and expectations. Even years later, the company still stands behind this claim. We consistently work on improving our service so that we can find the the most effective solution for our customers.


Our many years of experience in this industry have provided us with many long-term business relationships which we continuously cultivate. Strong relationships and our desire to continuously improve ourselves have allowed our company to flourish for many years.

Combining Creativity and Efficiency to Embody Usefulness. That is our Purpose.

Structural Design Services

  • State-of-the-art structural engineering design technology including 3D laser scanning, point cloud and BIM modelling.
  • Structural design in steel, reinforced concrete, timber framing and masonry.
  • Structural design of all types of building construction including commercial, institutional, heavy and light Industrial, multistory, residential, heavy equipment foundations.
  • Production of permit, tender, and construction documents.
  • Industry leading structural design and analysis software including finite element analysis. Suite of in-house developed software programs for the design of complex structures.

3D Scanning services

  • Our state-of-the-art 3D laser scanning technology transforms the way you visualize, design, and execute your projects.  
  • We provide high-resolution data capture tailored to complex geometries and challenging environments. 
  • Our 3D scanning services cater to Engineering and construction, Heritage Preservation, Damage Assessment, Infrastructure Reviews and Safety Enhancements. 
  • With our combined expertise in 3D scanning and BIM, J. P. Samuel & Associates Inc. delivers solutions tailored to your project’s unique needs.

Renovations and Additions

Evaluation of Existing Structures

  • We provide reviews of existing building structures to assess structural adequacy and stability
