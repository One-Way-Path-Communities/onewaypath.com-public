Wellness Advantage

We use Fitwel’s leading certification to build health for all, creating healthier buildings and communities that strengthen well-being.

Fitwel Precedents

Application of the Fitwel standard is positively impacting 34 million residents and 120 million employees in 24 million building units across North America. Research on its own developments by QuadReal, a multi-residential developer, demonstrates successful application of the standard across 10,000 residential units in Canada.

The business case for the Fitwel standard is backed by expert analysis of 7,000+ academic research studies.
